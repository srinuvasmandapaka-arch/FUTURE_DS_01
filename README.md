# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship Program.

The objective of this project is to analyze business sales data and provide actionable insights that can help businesses improve revenue, profitability, and overall performance.

The analysis focuses on:

* Revenue trends over time
* Top-selling products
* Category-wise performance
* Regional performance
* Profitability analysis
* Business recommendations

---

## Dataset

Dataset Used: Superstore Sales Dataset

The dataset contains information about:

* Orders
* Customers
* Products
* Categories
* Regions
* Sales
* Profit
* Discounts

Total Records: 9,994

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

Analyzed sales performance across different months to identify growth patterns and seasonality.

### 2. Top 10 Products by Sales

Identified products generating the highest revenue.

### 3. Category Performance

Compared sales and profit across product categories.

### 4. Regional Performance

Evaluated sales and profitability across regions.

### 5. Top Profitable States

Identified states contributing the highest profit.

## Key Insights

1. Certain products contribute significantly more revenue than others.
2. Sales performance varies across regions.
3. Some categories generate higher profit margins than others.
4. A small number of states contribute a major share of total profit.
5. Monthly sales trends reveal periods of higher business activity.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Product Strategy

Focus marketing and inventory efforts on top-performing products.

### Regional Growth

Invest more resources in high-performing regions while investigating challenges in low-performing regions.

### Category Optimization

Prioritize categories with strong profit margins and review underperforming categories.

### Profit Improvement

Reduce excessive discounting and optimize pricing strategies.

### Sales Forecasting

Use historical sales trends to improve inventory and resource planning.
